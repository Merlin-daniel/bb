Our Story

Built on the Belief That Business Setup Shouldn't Be Hard

La Douceur was founded in 2015 by a team of business consultants who had spent years watching entrepreneurs struggle with the same preventable problems — wrong jurisdiction, unexpected costs, delayed licenses, missed compliance deadlines. We knew there was a better way.

We started small, working with a handful of founders who needed someone they could actually trust to handle the details. Word spread. A decade later, we've helped over 800 businesses establish themselves across the UAE — from solo consultants setting up their first free zone company to multinational enterprises structuring complex holding arrangements.

What hasn't changed is the way we work. Every client gets direct access to their consultant, a clear picture of costs before any commitment, and a team that stays with them long after the setup is done.

What Drives Us

Our Mission & Values

We're not a license factory. We're a consultancy that cares about getting things right — for each client, every time. These are the principles we hold ourselves to.

Clarity First

We tell you exactly what something costs, how long it takes, and what can go wrong — before you commit. No hidden fees. No surprises.

Personal Service

You work with a named consultant throughout your engagement — not a helpdesk. We keep the relationship direct and the communication clear.

Accountability

When we take something on, it gets done. We track every deadline, follow up on every submission, and flag any issue the moment it appears.

Discretion

Business setup involves sensitive information. We treat every engagement with confidentiality and handle your data with the care it deserves.

Long-Term Thinking

We're not looking for a quick setup fee. We want to be your go-to partner as your business grows — and we structure our work around that.

Local Expertise

We live and work in the UAE. We know the authorities, the processes, and the nuances — and that knowledge saves our clients time and money.

Why Clients Choose Us

Not Just a Service Provider

A lot of consultancies in Dubai will take your documents, submit them, and move on. We work differently. From your first consultation to your third year of renewals, you deal with the same team — people who know your business, your structure, and your history.
