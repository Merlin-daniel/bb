Common Questions

Before You Write

How quickly will someone respond to my enquiry?

All enquiries submitted through our contact form are reviewed and replied to within one business day. Urgent matters can be directed to our WhatsApp line for a faster response.

Is the initial consultation really free?

Yes — the first consultation is complimentary with no obligation. You'll walk away with a clear picture of what your setup requires, how long it takes, and what it costs.

Can I work with La Douceur remotely?

Absolutely. Most of our clients manage the entire setup process remotely. We handle all in-person government appointments, document submissions, and authority visits on your behalf.

Which languages does your team work in?

Our advisors work fluently in English, Arabic, French, and Italian — allowing us to serve a truly international clientele without intermediary translation.

How is my information handled?

All client information is treated with the strictest confidentiality and is never shared with third parties without your explicit consent. Our processes comply with UAE data protection regulations.
