Common Questions

Pricing FAQs

Are government fees included in your prices?

No — our fees cover our consultancy and processing work only. Government and authority fees are billed at cost, itemised upfront.

Which free zone is right for my business?

It depends on your activity, visa needs, and budget. Your consultant will compare options — IFZA, RAKEZ, DMCC, etc.

How long does company formation take?

Free zone: 3–7 working days. Mainland: 10–15 working days depending on approvals.

Can I upgrade my plan after setup?

Yes. Add any service or upgrade to an ongoing support retainer at any point.

Do you offer payment plans?

For larger engagements, we can split fees across two milestones — 50% on engagement, 50% on license issue.

What does "direct consultant access" mean?

You speak directly to the person doing the work — not a call centre. Every client has a named consultant.
